## ‚≠ê Review Strategy

**How to Get Reviews:**

1. **Post-Event Follow-Up**
   - Send client a thank-you email 7 days after delivery
   - Include a direct link to your Google Business Profile review page
   - Keep it simple: "If you're happy with the work, we'd appreciate a review!"

2. **Review Link**
   - Get your direct review link from Google Business Profile dashboard
   - Format: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR_ID]/review`
   - Include in email signatures, invoices, thank-you emails

3. **What to Ask For**
   - Specific details (what event, what they liked)
   - Mention "professional," "fast turnaround," "easy to work with"
   - Authentic feedback (not scripted)

**Sample Review Request Email:**


## üöÄ Setup Steps

1. **Go to:** https://business.google.com
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Search** for "Core Visuals NYC" or "99 Wall Street New York"
4. If it exists, **claim it**. If not, **create new business**
5. **Verify** your business:
   - Option 1: Postcard to 99 Wall Street (takes 5-7 days)
   - Option 2: Phone verification (if available)
   - Option 3: Email verification (if domain is verified in Search Console)
6. **Complete profile:**
   - Add business description (use Version 1 above)
   - Add services (list from above)
   - Upload photos (10-15 minimum)
   - Set hours (Mon-Fri 9am-6pm)
   - Add attributes (appointment required, etc.)
7. **Publish** and wait for verification
