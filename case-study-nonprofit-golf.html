The Insight Most Nonprofits Miss

They think they're buying event documentation. A nice recap video to post and move on. But here's what you're actually creating: next year's recruitment tool.

This was a New Jersey nonprofit's first video initiative. Annual golf outing. 300+ participants. Celebrities, major donors, beneficiaries. Cherry auction. Speeches. The works.

They raised over $200,000 that day.

The real question wasn't "should we document this?" It was: "How do we get 30 more people to show up next year?"

What We Actually Filmed

The Setup

We weren't documenting the event. We were building a content library.

  • Grant application footage
